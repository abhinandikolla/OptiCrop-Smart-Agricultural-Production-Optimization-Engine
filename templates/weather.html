Key Weather Facts & Diagnostics

Temperature Influence on Photosynthesis

Every crop has an optimal thermal band. Ambient heat controls respiration rates and enzymatic reactions, determining the rate of dry-matter accumulation.

Humidity & Vapor Pressure Deficit

Relative air moisture regulates stomatal opening. High humidity limits plant sweating (transpiration), while low humidity causes plant dehydration and cell stress.

Rainfall Patterns & Irrigation Sync

Precise regional precipitation tracking determines natural water availability. Matching seasonal rainfall cycles with crop needs prevents soil erosion and root rot.
